Chihuahua

The Chihuahua is a small breed of dog known for its lively personality. It is the smallest recognized dog breed, named after the Mexican state of Chihuahua. They are popular pets due to their compact size and spirited nature.

Poodle

The Poodle is a highly intelligent and active breed of dog. It comes in three main sizes: standard, miniature, and toy. Poodles are known for their curly, non-shedding coats and are often regarded as one of the most elegant dog breeds.

Beagle

The Beagle is a friendly and curious breed of dog. It is known for its keen sense of smell and is often used in scent detection work. Beagles are medium-sized dogs with a short, easy-to-maintain coat and are popular as family pets.

German Shepherd

The German Shepherd is a working dog known for its intelligence and strength. It is often used in various roles, including search and rescue, police and military work, guide or service dog. They are loyal, protective, and excellent companions.
